Wellness and Healthy Living


A healthy lifestyle is important. It aims to enhance your physical, mental & socia well-being. Aside from keeping your body from chronic illness & long term diseases, it will improve your self-esteem, focus and will keep you motivated. Maintaining good habits will also improve your financial savings in the long run as you lessen frequent visit to the doctor due to illness.

Wellness is a practice of people doing healthy habits on a daily basis.
For some, it is not easy to make choices, and building healthy habits can truely be a challenge.
But if we can include small steps in our daily routine, we can improve our lifestyle a little better each day.

Be mindful of the things you do: the food you eat, your daily physical activities. It is also important to look into how you connect with other people, and how you manage stress.

Why start a healthy lifestyle?


A healthy lifestyle have a lot of benefits that affects our well-being.

•Improve physical health
•Improve mental health
•Save Money
•Prevent health issues
•Reduce Stress
•Boost your mood
•Improves Memory

Tips for Starters



•Start Slowly. No need to rush when starting to exercise, workout, or start a healthy living. Everyone has their own pace. You can change your lifestyle slowly until you can adjust to your new lifestyle.


•Eat Healthy and balanced meal. Have a diet composed of carbohydrates, protein, fiber, vitamin and mineral-rich foods.

•Eat Regularly. You need to get enough calories and nutrients that you will need to charge yourself up for the day.


•Stay Hydrated. Drinking 4-8 glasses of water a day.


•Exercise Regularly. To make your body stronger, you have to be more active. You can exercise everywhere. You can do it inside your house or outdoors.


•Get sufficient and Quality sleep. a good sleep brings us lots of energy, helps us focus more, and brings us in a good mood.


•Avoid dangerous substances. Avoid tobacco and alcohol products as is ruins your wellbeing.


•Exercise your brain. Wellness and healthy living doesn`t only focus on physical health. Mental health is also important. Try solving some puzzles, play board games, read a book or spend time outdoors.


•Discipline. Self-discipline makes you more focus and gives drive to accomplish your goal.


•Visit your doctor regularly. You should see a doctor to keep track of your health.


•Good Personal Hygiene. One of the best way to prevent diseases and keeping your body clean. It is also important for maintaining both physical and mental health.
